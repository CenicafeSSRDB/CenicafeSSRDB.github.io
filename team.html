Who we are?


Cenicafé SSRdb, is an initiative of the Plant Pathology (Gustavo Marín-Ramírez) and Plant Breeding (Carlos Ernesto Maldonado Londoño) disciplines of the National Coffee Research Centre - Cenicafé-FNC, to facilitate the distribution of information related to microsatellite markers useful in genome mapping, characterisation of genetic material, population genetics and evolutionary studies in the Patosystem. Cenicafé-FNC, to facilitate the distribution of information related to microsatellite markers useful in genome mapping, characterisation of genetic material, population genetics and evolutionary studies in the Hemileia-Coffea Patosystem. In addition to promoting open science and reproducible research practices in plant pathology.
